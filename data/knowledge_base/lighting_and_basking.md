# Lighting And Basking

## UVB Light

UVB lighting supports calcium metabolism and shell health. The light should be appropriate for reptiles and positioned according to manufacturer guidance.

## UVA And Heat

Heat lamps help create a warm basking spot. UVA may support natural activity, but UVB and heat placement are the most important beginner setup concerns.

## Basking Temperature

The basking area should be warm enough to encourage drying and thermoregulation. Avoid overheating and always provide access back to water.

## Distance And Barriers

UVB output depends on distance and barriers. Glass or acrylic can block UVB, so the lamp setup should follow product instructions.

## Lighting Schedule

A regular day-night light schedule helps maintain routine. Avoid leaving bright lights on continuously.

## Bulb Replacement

UVB bulbs lose output over time even if they still produce visible light. Replace bulbs according to manufacturer recommendations.

# Feeding

## Juvenile Turtles

Juvenile turtles often need more frequent feeding and more protein than adults, but overfeeding can still cause health and water quality problems.

## Adult Turtles

Adult turtles usually need a more balanced diet with more plant matter, depending on species. Feeding frequency may be lower than for juveniles.

## Commercial Pellets

Quality turtle pellets can be a useful diet base. Choose pellets appropriate for the turtle's size and species.

## Vegetables And Plants

Many aquatic turtles benefit from safe leafy greens or aquatic plants. Species needs differ, so diet should not rely on one food only.

## Animal Protein

Animal protein may be useful, especially for young turtles or certain species, but too much can contribute to imbalance and dirty water.

## Calcium

Calcium sources and UVB lighting are both important for shell and bone health.

## Overfeeding Risks

Overfeeding can foul water, cause obesity, and create long-term care problems. Remove leftovers after feeding.

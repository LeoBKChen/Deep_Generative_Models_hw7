# Water Quality

## Filtration

Turtles produce heavy waste, so filtration should be stronger than what is used for many fish tanks of the same size. Canister filters or strong external filters are often useful for aquatic turtle setups.

## Partial Water Changes

Regular partial water changes help control waste. Replace part of the water instead of fully resetting the tank unless there is a serious contamination issue.

## Dechlorination

Tap water should be dechlorinated when required. Chlorine and chloramine may irritate aquatic animals and affect beneficial bacteria.

## Ammonia, Nitrite, And Nitrate

Ammonia and nitrite should be kept near zero in a mature aquarium. Nitrate should be controlled with water changes and maintenance.

## Water Temperature

Water temperature should match the species. Sudden temperature changes can stress turtles.

## Feeding Cleanup

Remove leftover food after feeding. Feeding in a separate container may help reduce tank waste for some keepers.

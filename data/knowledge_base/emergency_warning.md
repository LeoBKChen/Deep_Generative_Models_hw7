# Emergency Warning

TurtleCare AI is an educational husbandry assistant and does not provide emergency veterinary care.

## Seek Professional Help

Consult a qualified reptile veterinarian for severe, persistent, or rapidly worsening symptoms.

Warning signs include:

- Serious injury.
- Bleeding.
- Long-term refusal to eat.
- Abnormal floating or imbalance.
- Noisy breathing or open-mouth breathing.
- Eyes unable to open.
- Severe shell or skin infection.
- Extreme weakness or inability to move normally.

## Husbandry Checks While Seeking Help

Check water quality, temperature, basking access, UVB lighting, and whether tank mates are causing stress or injury. These checks do not replace veterinary care.

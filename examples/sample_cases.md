# Sample Cases

## Case 1: Q&A

- Species: Taiwan stripe-necked turtle
- Shell length: 8 cm
- Question: Does my turtle need a UVB light?

Expected behavior:

- Retrieve lighting and basking knowledge.
- Explain UVB importance.
- Avoid medical diagnosis.

## Case 2: Environment Diagnosis

- Species: Red-eared slider
- Number of turtles: 1
- Shell length: 10 cm
- Tank: 60 x 35 x 40 cm
- Water depth: 20 cm
- Basking area: No
- UVB light: No
- Filtration: weak internal filter
- Feeding: pellets and dried shrimp

Expected behavior:

- Flag basking area risk.
- Flag UVB risk.
- Flag filtration and feeding concerns.
- Generate improvement checklist.

## Case 3: Tank Design Prompt

- Species: Musk turtle
- Tank: 75 x 45 x 45 cm
- Water depth: 18 cm
- Style: natural clean indoor aquarium
- Required elements: dry basking platform, gentle ramp, filter, hiding area
- Avoid elements: sharp rocks, tiny gravel

Expected behavior:

- Generate English diffusion-ready prompt.
- Generate negative prompt.
- Keep image generation optional.
